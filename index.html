NBA 2K

Intro

NBA 2k is a game based off of the sport game Basketball and this game has all the NBA stars in the game and as of recent added the WNBA players into the game. NBA 2K has now been out for 24 years the first one coming out in the 1999 november and the first cover athlete being NBA 76ers superstar Allen Iverson and over the years they have had multiple other athletes being on the cover like Micheal Jordan, Lebron James, Kobe Bryant and many other stars.

Game Modes

There are multiple game modes in NBA 2k23 but the most popular ones are MyCareer, MyTeam, and MyLeague and in all of these game modes you have a lot control of what you can do in the MyCareer game mode you have control of what type of player you want to make and how good they can be style of play, In MyTeam you can create your own team with the characters that are available in the game mode, and in Myleague you have the most control of what to do just like MyTeam you can create your own team but in MyLeague you can create a chracter to add into the game and you are in control of nba teams and you can control 1 team or all 30 teams in the NBA.

MyCareer

MyCareer is a game mode where you can make your own nba player that plays in NBA and there is a story and you choose how your story goes you can choose out all the big shoe brands which one you want to go with you can choose what college you gp to and pick what team you get drafted by, just like the NBA you can play 82 games earn the in game virtual currency for each game you play and depending on which difficulty you play on you can make more or less VC(virtual currency) and you can make in game endoresment deals to make more VC to upgrade your player.

player creation, before you can start your MyCareer experience you have to make a player and you can make this player to your choosing, you can make a paint dominant center like Shaquile O'Neal, a point guard three point specialist like Stephen Curry, an all around small forward like Lebron James, a shot creating shooting guard like James Harden, or an stretch forward like Dirk Nowitzki.

Once you make a player and complete the college story that is realativly short you can access the online part of MyCareer where you can play up against other people in a 1v1 match, a 2v2 match, and a 3v3 match, there is also a rec where you can play 5v5.

MyTeam

MyTeam is a mode where you can make your own team with characters in the game you can choose a coach there are play books from every team and some from past teams and they have different overalls they correlate to a element from the world bronze(worst), silver, gold, emerald, blue saphire, ruby, amythest, diamond pink diamond, galaxy opal, and dark matter(best) but the playbooks, shoes, and contracts only go up to diamond.

There is also an online feature where you can play up against other people and see whos team is the better one there are offline modes where you can play against an A.I. to get better and there are challenges to get good rewards, to get players there is an pack market where you can buy VC(virtual Currency) to open packs or you can use MyTeam points that you earn from playing games.

MyLeague

MyLeague is kind of like MyTeam but you have more control over things like how many games are in a season, you can create and add teams, you can make super teams you've always wanted to see you can play in the old era with Micheal Jordan, Magic Johnson, and Larry Bird, you can add old players into our era and see how they would play in our era, you can download MyLeagues other people made with current highschool stars in the NBA you can make your own player and add him to a team or you can add him to the draft and there is still much more to explore about MyLeague.
